Summary of Latitude Data Analysis

This project was to show the relationship between latitude of a city and weather variables. The OpenWeatherMap API was used to collect weather data
on 04/12/2020 from 546 random cities from around the globe to compare the variables: max temperature, humidity, percent of clouds and wind speed.
The following visualizations are plots for each variable. Also included is a table of the raw data.

The above scatter plot is a representation of all the cities included, plotted by latitude and longitude.
It resembles the continents of the world, verifying the data was well distributed.
